I am Collaboratrice Scientifique II at the Département de Physique Théorique, Université de Genève, Geneva, Switzerland. I am also the principal investigator of the Swiss National Science Foundation Ambizione Grant for my project "Probing the Universe: through reionization and beyond".

My main areas of interest are cosmology, cosmic neutral hydrogen, structure formation in the universe and the intergalactic medium. I have also worked previously on the weak lensing of the cosmic microwave background, and on several areas in theoretical physics.
